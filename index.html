Sample Pad

This is an interactive web app version of a percussion instrument. This concept is inspired by the Alesis Strike Multipad.

Each key pad corresponds with different sound and keyboard key. PLEASE NOTE: clicking a pad will not play any sound.

3 backing tracks have been provided for you to play along with. Toggle ON will play the track and toggle OFF will pause the track.
To replay or restart a track, please refresh the page. You can play multiple tracks at the same time.
